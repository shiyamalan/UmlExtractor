In a shop system, a customer can not order more than one items.
Customer details must record the name and location.
A customer can send and receive the order using the system.
The customer can order in two types.
Orders are special order and normal order.
An order can be confirmed and closed by the customer.
The special order only can dispose the items.
Normal order can dispose and receive the items.
A regular customer is given a shopping_card.
The shopping_card holds his name, User_Id and points.
A customer has the shopping_card is given a discount for his orders.
Employee's name, address, ic_number and employee_no are maintained by the system.
Manager is an employee responsible for the shop.
Employees have swipe_card to register their attendance.
